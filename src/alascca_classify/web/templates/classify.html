About

The ALASCCA trial (Martling et al., NEJM 2025) showed that adjuvant low-dose aspirin (160 mg daily for 3 years) significantly reduces recurrence in resected stage I–III colorectal cancer with somatic PI3K pathway alterations. Group A (PIK3CA exon 9/20 hotspots) and Group B (other PI3K pathway alterations in PIK3CA, PIK3R1, or PTEN) both showed benefit (combined HR 0.45). Approximately 37% of CRC patients harbor these alterations. See Methods for details.
